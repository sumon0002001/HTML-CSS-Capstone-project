ULTRA EUROPE 2021

Ultra Europe is a huge Croatian dance music festival and the European edition of the global event brand, held each summer in the Croatian city of Split. Returning to the city after a year off, Ultra Europe is back in July 2021 for another week of EDM, house, techno and trance parties, all spread across huge main stages, clubs, beaches and more.

2021.07.09(FRI) ~ 11(SUN)

@Mladeži Park, Split, Croatia
